Before you book

The questions founders ask first.

Why one person instead of an agency?

With an agency, your strategist, media buyer, and creative team are different people — and feedback dies between them. I run the whole loop, so every signal from Ads Manager shapes the next creative and the next budget the same week. One brain, one feedback loop.

What do you need from me to start?

Access to your Shopify store and Meta ad account, your current offers and margins, and any brand assets you already have. If you've run ads before, I'll want to see what's been tested. We cover all of it on the first call.

How soon can campaigns go live?

After the strategy call, the first creative sprint produces a batch of short-form ads and statics around the angles we agree on. Campaigns launch with structured tests as soon as that batch is ready — usually within the first week or two.

Do you work with my store size or niche?

I focus on Shopify brands ready to put real budget behind growth — a product that's already selling and a founder who wants a tighter creative-and-ads engine behind it. Not sure you're a fit? Book the call and I'll tell you straight.

How does pricing work?

Every engagement starts with a strategy call — no pitch deck. From there I scope the work to your stage and goals, so pricing reflects what you actually need. You'll know exactly what you're paying for before we start.
